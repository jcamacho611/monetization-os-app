# Zumi Brand Brief

## Name
Zumi

## Slogan
Nothing slips. Everything closes.

## Positioning
Zumi is an adaptive revenue operating system for local service businesses. It turns missed leads into booked jobs automatically by adapting follow-up cadence, channel mix, and messaging to the way each business actually sells.

## Logo Meaning
The mark combines a loop and a hidden arrow. The loop represents continuity, follow-up, and control. The arrow represents recovered momentum, closed jobs, and revenue moving forward.

## Tone Of Voice
- Premium
- Confident
- Minimal
- Sharp
- Outcome-driven

## Color Palette
- Deep black: `#0A0A0A`
- Graphite: `#14171C`
- Gunmetal: `#1A1D23`
- Chrome: `#D6DBE1`
- Steel blue accent: `#7F909F`
- Electric frost accent: `#93E1FF`

## Typography Direction
Use clean sans-serif typography for interface and navigation, with restrained high-contrast editorial display type for major headlines. The overall feel should stay sleek, premium, and highly legible.
